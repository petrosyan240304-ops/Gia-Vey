Aeralis is quiet, atmospheric, and slightly mysterious. The most common sounds are wind moving through open spaces, distant echoes, and soft vibrations coming from the floating land itself.

There are no crowded streets or loud city noises. The weather is cool and cloudy, and the repeating natural sounds create a peaceful but haunting atmosphere.

This page presents the Audio Assignment and explores the sound environment of Gia Vey’s world.
