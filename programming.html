Gia has the ability to bend space around her and move instantly between locations. Instead of disappearing suddenly, the air around her shifts and folds, allowing her to step out of one place and appear in another.

She can also partially blend into fog, light, and shadow, making her presence feel almost unreal. Her power is subtle and controlled, and she uses it to observe, reposition, and understand her surroundings more deeply.

This page presents the Programming Assignment and explores Gia’s unique transformative ability.
